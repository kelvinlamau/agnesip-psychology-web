Couple Therapy
Face to face · Sydney Telehealth · Australia wide
Gottman Method

Developed by Drs John and Julie Gottman from decades of relationship research, the Gottman Method focuses on deepening friendship, managing conflict constructively, and building a shared sense of meaning. It provides practical, science-backed tools to help couples move from gridlock to dialogue.

Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)

EFT for couples, developed by Dr Sue Johnson, works by identifying and transforming the negative interaction cycles that keep partners feeling disconnected. By accessing the deeper emotional needs beneath conflict, EFT helps couples create a more secure and lasting bond.
