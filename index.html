About Agnes

A psychologist who meets you with warmth and rigour.

Agnes Ip is a registered clinical psychologist with over 15 years of experience supporting individuals through anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, and identity challenges. Her approach weaves together the best of evidence-based practice with genuine human warmth.

Trained in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), and trauma-informed approaches, Agnes brings both precision and compassion to every session. She is particularly drawn to working with clients navigating cultural identity, perfectionism, and the quiet exhaustion of high-functioning anxiety.

Agnes completed her clinical training in Hong Kong and Australia, and holds registration with the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA). She is a member of the Australian Psychological Society (APS).
