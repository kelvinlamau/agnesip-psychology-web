About Agnes

Hello, this is Agnes. I am a registered clinical psychologist in Australia and Hong Kong. I provide psychological assessments and treatment to clients of all ages, both in-person in Sydney as well as by telehealth.

My approach is collaborative, culturally-sensitive, and grounded in evidence-based treatments. My aim is to help clients build lives and relationships that are fulfilling, by guiding them to connect with what matters most and by equipping them with relevant psychological skills.
